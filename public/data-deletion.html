Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Townsville Business Hub is committed to respecting your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of your data from our systems, including any data collected via Facebook Login or other integrations.

1. What Data We Hold

When you use our Service — including logging in via Facebook — we may store:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your Facebook User ID (if connected via Facebook Login)
  • Any business listings, posts, or content you submitted
  • Usage logs and analytics associated with your account

2. How to Request Data Deletion

📧 To request deletion of your data, email us at: admin@townsvillebusinesshub.online

Subject line: Data Deletion Request

Please include the following in your request:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your Facebook User ID (if applicable — found in your Facebook account settings)
  • A brief description of what data you'd like deleted

3. What Happens After You Request

  • We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days.
  • Your data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days of your request.
  • We will send you a confirmation email once deletion is complete.
  • Some data may be retained if required by law (e.g. financial records) — we will inform you of any exceptions.

4. Facebook-Specific Data Deletion

If you connected your Facebook account to our Service, you can also revoke our app's access directly from Facebook:

  • Go to Facebook Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites
  • Find Townsville Business Hub and click Remove
  • This revokes our access token immediately

After revoking access on Facebook, please also send us an email at the address above to ensure all stored data is fully purged from our servers.

5. Contact Us

For any questions about data deletion, please contact:

  • Email: admin@townsvillebusinesshub.online
  • Website: townsvillebusinesshub.online
  • Location: Townsville, QLD, Australia
